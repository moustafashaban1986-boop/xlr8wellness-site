Return & Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair, transparent, and customer-first service policies across all xlr8well services in the UAE.

Effective: 1 January 2025 Last updated: 28 May 2026 Jurisdiction: United Arab Emirates
📋
Policy Overview
Applies to all xlr8well services in the UAE

xlr8well, operated by Bloom Healthcare LLC, is a DHA-licensed healthcare and wellness marketplace delivering services across the UAE. This policy governs refunds, returns, and cancellations for all services and products booked or purchased through xlr8well.life.

We are committed to delivering safe, high-quality healthcare and wellness services. If you are not satisfied with your experience, we encourage you to contact us — we will do our best to resolve your concern fairly.

Summary: We accept returns for defective products. We offer refunds for services cancelled within the required notice period, and we review all complaints related to service quality on a case-by-case basis.
💉
Healthcare & Wellness Services
IV therapy, blood testing, consultations, holistic treatments
Scenario Outcome
Cancellation 24+ hours before appointment Full refund
Cancellation 12–24 hours before appointment 50% refund
Cancellation less than 12 hours before appointment No refund
No-show without cancellation No refund
Service not delivered due to xlr8well error Full refund or rescheduled at no cost
Service delivered but unsatisfactory quality Reviewed case by case — contact us
Medical contraindication preventing service Full refund

Note: Once an IV therapy, blood test, or clinical treatment has commenced, it is considered delivered. Refunds are not available for services already in progress except where a practitioner error or product defect is identified.

📦
Products & Medical Kits
Wellness kits, supplements, at-home testing kits, consumables

xlr8well accepts returns for physical products under the following conditions:

Product Type Return Accepted Condition
Defective or damaged product on arrival Yes Must be reported within 48 hours of receipt with photo evidence
Wrong item delivered Yes Full exchange or refund within 7 days
Unopened, sealed product (change of mind) Yes Within 7 days of purchase, original packaging required
Opened wellness supplements or consumables No Hygiene and safety reasons
Used at-home testing kits No Medical safety — once activated, kits cannot be returned
Digital or subscription products No Non-refundable once accessed

To initiate a product return, contact us at info@xlr8well.com with your order number, a description of the issue, and photos where applicable.

🔄
Exchanges
Product and service exchanges

We accept exchanges for:

  • Defective products — exchanged for the same item at no cost
  • Wrong item delivered — exchanged for the correct item at no cost
  • Unopened products within 7 days — exchanged for an alternative product of equal or greater value (price difference applies)

Service appointments may be rescheduled free of charge with a minimum of 24 hours' notice. Rescheduling with less than 24 hours' notice is subject to a rebooking fee.

💳
Refund Processing
How and when refunds are issued

Approved refunds are processed as follows:

  • Credit/debit card payments: Refunded to the original card within 5–10 business days
  • Bank transfer payments: Refunded via bank transfer within 7–14 business days
  • Wallet or credit balance: Credited to your xlr8well account within 24–48 hours
Refund timelines are subject to your bank's processing times. xlr8well is not responsible for delays caused by financial institutions.

How to Request a Return or Refund
Step-by-step process
  1. Email info@xlr8well.com with the subject line "Return/Refund Request — [Your Order Number]"
  2. Include your full name, contact number, order or booking reference
  3. Describe the reason for your request clearly
  4. Attach photos or evidence if relevant (damaged products, wrong items, etc.)
  5. Our team will respond within 2 business days with a decision or next steps

UAE consumer protection framework

Nothing in this policy limits your rights under UAE consumer protection legislation. If you have an unresolved complaint, you may contact the UAE Ministry of Economy Consumer Protection Department or the Dubai Health Authority.
